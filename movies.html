Favorite Movies

Two of my favorite films are Interstellar and Oppenheimer. Both explore science, humanity, and emotion through breathtaking storytelling and visuals.

Interstellar

Why I like it: The movie blends science, space exploration, and emotional depth — a perfect combination of logic and heart.

  • Director: Christopher Nolan
  • Released: 2014
  • Genre: Sci-Fi / Adventure

Oppenheimer

Why I like it: It’s an intense portrayal of ambition, ethics, and the consequences of scientific discovery.

  • Director: Christopher Nolan
  • Released: 2023
  • Genre: Biography / Drama
